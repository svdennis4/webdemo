Directions

If you are driving south on I-71:

Turn left at the end of the ramp on to Highland Ave.

Turn left onto Orkney Ave. (just after you cross Madison) - we will be immediately on the right.

Use exit 8a, Ridge Rd. South.

Turn right at the end of the ramp on to Ridge Rd.

Turn left onto Orkney Ave. (just after you cross Madison) - we will be immediately on the right.
If you are driving east on 562 (the Norwood Lateral):
