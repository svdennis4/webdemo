About Climb Time

The first Climb Time opened in Blue Ash back in 1993! In 2019, a second Climb Time location was opened in Oakley.

Climb Time Oakley is a 45ft tall facility with lead climbing, top roping, speed climbing, and of course bouldering. We are open to beginner, intermediate, and advanced climbers. Our pro shop provides climbing gear, clothing, books, and accessories.
